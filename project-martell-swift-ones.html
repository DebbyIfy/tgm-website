PR Amplification · Narrative Strategy · Media Placement

Setting the Pace for Swift Ones.

PR amplification and narrative strategy for the Swift Ones campaign by Martell.

ClientMartell Swift Ones
SectorConsumer, Beverage
CampaignSwift Diaries — a four-part series featuring Young Jonn, Timini Egbuson, Chef Eros, and Sandrah Tubobereni
ChallengeAmplify the stories of four trailblazers across music, film, culinary, and fashion, positioning Martell as a brand that celebrates audacity and the pursuit of excellence.
WatchCampaign video

TGM's Role

That Good Media managed the PR amplification and narrative strategy for the entire Swift Ones campaign. This included selecting the right media platforms, drafting and distributing press releases and feature articles for each Swift Diaries episode, and amplifying promotional assets across targeted online blogs and social media channels.

The campaign was rolled out in phases — starting with the announcement, then individual amplification for each Swift One (Timini Egbuson, Chef Eros, Sandrah Tubobereni, and Young Jonn), and closing with a full recap across all episodes.

Key Deliverables

Media platform selection and strategy
Press releases and feature articles per episode
Blog placements (Punch, Business Day, Bella Naija, Nollywire, Culture Custodian, Eat Drink Lagos, NotJustOk, TooXclusive)
Social media amplification across Instagram blogs
Sponsored ad coordination (Nollywire IG + FB)
Phased rollout: announcement, per-talent, recap
