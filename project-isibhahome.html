Bringing a Vision to Life Through Meaningful Experiences.

PR, event strategy, and guest curation for a lifestyle brand launch in Lagos.

ClientIsibhaho'me
SectorLifestyle, Events
ServicesPR, Event Planning, Guest Curation
ChallengeFor the launch of Isibhaho'me, the goal was to create more than an event — an introduction that felt intentional, memorable, and worth talking about. Every touchpoint had to reflect the vision while bringing together the right people to shape the experience.

TGM's Role

We led the launch through a blend of PR, event strategy, planning, and guest curation. Working closely with the client, we mapped out an experience designed to create excitement before the event and meaningful engagement during it.

From shaping the event flow and managing execution to building a guest list that balanced influence, relevance, and community, we ensured every detail worked toward one goal: a launch people would remember.

The result was more than a successful event. It was a strong debut that generated buzz, created connection, and gave Isibhaho'me an unforgettable start.

Key Deliverables

PR strategy and media outreach
Launch event concept and planning
Guest list curation and management
Event flow design and execution
Influencer and community engagement
Brand experience coordination
