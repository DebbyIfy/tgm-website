About That Good Media

That Good Media is a PR, branding and talent agency based in Lagos, offering event and growth solutions to brands of all sizes.

Our story

Connecting possibilities

Our journey started nearly a decade ago, when we launched That Good Media with one central purpose: to help people and brands speak their deepest truth with confidence.

Crafting excellence

Since then, we have discovered newer ways to help brands stand out and survive storms with an expanding range of creative and strategic solutions.
Armed with an inclusive team of talented visionaries and 50+ years of combined industry experience, we provide the clarity and direction that positions brands for relevance, growth, and long-term impact.

Exceeding expectations

From large-scale global businesses to cultural movements and independent initiatives, our clinical approach to tackling complex challenges continues to earn us long-term value, as we continue to deliver superior value with every project.
