Personal Branding

Positioning a Modern African Royal Voice.

Personal branding for Olori Atuwatse III.

ClientOlori Atuwatse III
SectorPublic Figure / Leadership
ChallengeBuild a cohesive and distinctive public identity that reflects a modern African royal figure while expanding visibility, cultural relevance, and influence across traditional and digital platforms.

TGM's Role

We positioned Olori Atuwatse III as a pan-African thought leader by shaping a clear narrative around her multifaceted identity and contributions. Through a strategic communications approach across media and digital channels, we created consistency in how her story, values, and impact were experienced by audiences.

By highlighting her work across law, entrepreneurship, and community development, we strengthened public perception and built broader recognition that extended beyond traditional expectations of royal visibility.

Key Deliverables

Pan-African thought leader positioning
Strategic communications across media and digital
Law, entrepreneurship, and community narrative
Public perception management
Cross-sector visibility (law, business, community)
Consistent brand storytelling
